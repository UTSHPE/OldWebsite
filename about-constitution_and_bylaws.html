Why Join SHPE?

National Benefits

Become a SHPE National member here - Join Now

  • More than $2 million in scholarship offerings.
  • Internship, fellowships & co-op opportunities.
  • Exclusive access to the SHPE Career Center where you can share your resume with top employers and search for job openings.
  • Tips on résumé writing and interview skills.
  • Professional, career, & leadership training offered at our various events:
    • SHPE Conference
    • National Institute for Leadership Advancement (NILA)
    • Graduate Leadership Institute (GLI)
    • Regional Leadership Development Conferences (RLDC)
  • Competition opportunities to test and refine your academic and technical talent
  • Access to the SHPE Foundation GRE/GRAD Lab to guide you through the graduate school application process.
  • Access to the SHPE Foundation Graduate Institute, an intensive program filled seminars addressing the needs of Graduate Students.
  • Lifelong networking opportunities with high profile professionals, fellow students, corporate representatives and SHPE leaders.
  • Discounted registration to SHPE National, Regional & Local events.
  • Complimentary SHPE stoles upon graduation.
  • Complimentary subscriptions to SHPE Magazine and Hispanic Business Magazine.
  • Eligible to nominate or be nominated for SHPE's most prestigious national awards.
  • Become part of the SHPE Familia and find support whereever you go with over 250 chapters and 10,000 members across the nation.

Local Benefits


National Organization:

Membership of national organization which allows for travel opportunities, as well as scholarships.


Leadership Development:

By being an active member in the chapter, you are eligible to run for an executive board position, where you will hone and develop organizational skills needed in the workplace.


​Engineering Projects:

From computer science to mechanical engineering, you’ll use many techniques and concepts from a broad spectrum of disciplines to complete team and chapter projects; some of which may be used in competitions with other chapters.


Networking:

In SHPE, we will take trips to conferences and offices where you will be able to meet other engineers and engineering students, many of whom may be a future employer.


Transition:

Your path to becoming a career won’t end after your time at Wright College. Through campus visits, we will establish relationships with chapters that you may later join when you transfer. Why not make friends now, and make the transition a bit easier?


Recognition:

SHPE is regarded by many employers as a great stepping stone for a student interested in a career in engineering.
